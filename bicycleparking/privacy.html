Privacy Policy

Parking à vélo
Last updated: January 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Parking à vélo ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

By using Parking à vélo, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Location Data

Parking à vélo requires access to your device's location to help you find nearby bicycle parking spots. We collect and process your location data only when:

  • You grant permission to access your device's location services
  • You actively use the app to search for parking spots

Your location data is used solely to provide the core functionality of the app and is not stored on our servers or shared with third parties.

Device Information

We may collect certain device information automatically, including:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App version and usage statistics
  • Crash reports and error logs (if applicable)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve the app's core functionality (finding bicycle parking spots)
  • Display relevant parking locations based on your current location
  • Improve app performance and fix technical issues
  • Ensure the app's security and prevent abuse

3. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage: Your location data is processed locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers or stored remotely.

Third-Party Services: We may use third-party services (such as Google Play Services for location services) that have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

Security: We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your information, though no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

4. Location Permissions

Parking à vélo requires location permissions to function properly. You can:

  • Grant location access when prompted by the app
  • Revoke location permissions at any time through your device's settings
  • Note that revoking location permissions will limit the app's ability to find nearby parking spots

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share aggregated, anonymized data for analytical purposes, but this data cannot be used to identify you personally.

6. Children's Privacy

Parking à vélo is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • The right to access your personal data
  • The right to request deletion of your data
  • The right to object to processing of your data
  • The right to data portability

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the Contact section below.
