Medium Android

Medium is a free, open platform for storytelling and idea sharing that allows individuals to publish and share their ideas, thoughts, and experiences with a wider audience. The Medium Android app allows users to access and engage with the content on the Medium platform from their Android devices.

With the app, users can easily discover new stories and ideas from a range of topics, including technology, business, health, and more. They can also customize their feed to see content that is most relevant to their interests and follow their favorite writers and publications.

In addition to reading and discovering content, users can also use the app to write and publish their own stories on the platform. The app includes a variety of formatting tools and features to help users create compelling and visually appealing content.

Users can also save stories to read later and share them with their friends and followers on other social media platforms. They can also leave comments and engage with the writers and other readers through the app's built-in commenting system.

Overall, the Medium Android app is a powerful tool for discovering, reading, and writing about the things that matter most to you.

Parking à vélo Android

Parking à vélo is a mobile application that helps users find and locate bike parking facilities in their city or town. The app is designed for people who use bikes as a primary mode of transportation and need a safe and secure place to park their bike when they are not riding it.

The app includes a map feature that shows the location of bike parking facilities in the user's area, along with information about the type of facility (e.g. racks, lockers, etc.), the number of spaces available, and any applicable fees.

Parking à vélo is a useful tool for people who rely on bikes as their primary mode of transportation, helping them find convenient and secure places to park their bikes when they are not in use.

Yubo Android

Yubo is a social networking app that is designed for people to make new friends, discover new cultures, and connect with like-minded individuals.

The app has a variety of features that allow users to interact with each other, including the ability to create and join live streaming rooms, send private messages, and share photos and videos. Users can also create and join groups based on their interests, and participate in challenges and quizzes.

One of the main draws of Yubo is its emphasis on connecting users with others who have similar interests. The app allows users to create profiles that include information about their hobbies, passions, and goals, and it uses this information to suggest potential friends and groups for users to join.

In addition to its social networking features, Yubo also has a number of safety measures in place to ensure that users have a positive and secure experience. These measures include strict community guidelines, moderated live streams, and the ability to report and block other users.

Overall, Yubo is a fun and interactive way for people to connect with others and discover new things.

WDMTG Website

Where does my tweet go? is a web service that allow to measure the spread of tweets around the world. The "SpreakRank" algorithm compute a score between tweets and retweets and define the velocity of your tweet. You can visualize the spread of a tweet on a graph.

Understanding the relationship between online word-of-mouth conversations and true engagement isn’t simple. Different patterns emerge with different types of content and different people involved.

Features:

  • 01 Track : You can track any trend, keywords or accounts. By tracking multiple elements you’ll create a feed that will be constantly updated with relevant tweets, smartly selected by our SpreadRank algorithm.
  • 02 Watch : Once your Custom feed is set up, you can bmdl-gridse tweets through an enriched timeline. The SpreadRank (orange bar) allows you to quickly discern the hot tweets and the hot topics. You can also define alerts based on the SpreadRank for each of your feed.
  • 03 Analyze : By means of an elegant 3D and real time data visualization, we enable you to observe the structure and the understanding of a message’s journey (i.e. influencers, trends).

Drivy Android

Drivy is the first peer-to-peer car sharing web and mobile app in France. Car owners can cover the cost generated by the car thanks to the rental of them personal car, and drivers can rent a car at a low price. The service includes an insurance that covers cars during rental.

The application embeds the following features:

  • For drivers:
    • Find the best car at the corner! (Location).
    • Contact the owner in one click (by phone or email), get agreement and book the owner car.
    • Receive real-time notifications.
    • Ask for rental extension with one click.
  • For owners:
    • Register your car for free takes in less than a minute.
    • Manage your rentals: accept, decline, or contact the driver.
    • Receive real-time notifications.﻿
